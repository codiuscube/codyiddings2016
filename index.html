About me

I'm an island boy from Kaua‘i.

I studied, worked and lived in San Diego in 10 years, and I am now experiencing New Zealand.

I am an established designer (with a strong foundation of print and interactive) focusing on user experience and product design, shifting my passion towards experience design.

As we move away from screens, a lot of our interfaces will have to become more automatic, anticipatory, and predictive. Designers might have to design in databases, or lookup tables, or spreadsheets. We will have to become experts in science, biology, and psychology.

This quote by Andy Goodman has changed the way I looked at my career, provoking a shift in mindset from Graphic Design towards Human-Centered Design. I still love my design and prototyping tools but this new mindset challenges and excites me far more than the traditional design career.

Experience & Skills

Thought Leadership

My entreprenenerial spirit pushes me to constantly lead and pursue new ideas. I absolutely love leading my team in new endeavors with tools or skill sets, preparing talks for inspiration, and being a part of the ever-growing design community.

Business Development & Ideation

My passion and energy consistently pushes seeds of ideas and problems into marketable products and solutions. For this reason, many people see me as a great partner in their business ventures.

UX & Product Strategy

Logical, systematic, data-informed thinking fuels my efforts when designing products. I’m able to use any medium to convey these ideas including sketches, whiteboarding, Keynote, Axure, Invision and more.

Experience Design

I often ask this question when a client requests a new website or app: “Are you sure that’s going to help? What if the solution to your problem isn’t digital?” The core of experience design looks at users empathically. This is truly the future of CX & UX design.

UI & Visual Design

My background as a systematic Graphic Designer empowers me to not just convey great ideas, but take them all the way through to development. From UI to marketing to print to branding, I’ve done it all.

Prototyping & Animation

Conveying ideas isn’t always static. In fact, it’s often a dynamic experience when communicating flows, interaction, and animations. To do this, I use Invision, Principle, Pixate, and more. I’m currently learning Framer.
